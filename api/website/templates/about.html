About the Game!

Starting

Though our version has a different look to the beloved original, CS Jeopardy is still the game you all know and love!

Start a game by inviting friends to your session starting when everyone's ready.

Playing the game

As the host, you will start things off by selecting the category and difficulty of the question. You and the other players will be taken into a screen with the question.
(img for game board)
You will have 20 seconds to decide and choose your answer from the listed options.

Getting it right

Get it right, get the value added to your total!
(img of question screen; include correct answer message)
You then get to select the next question, continuing your control of the game!

Getting it wrong

Get it wrong, that same amount will get deducted from your total!
(img of question screen; include wrong answer message)
The other players will have the chance to answer and gain control of the game!

Opponent opportunity

When the current player gets the quesiton wrong, you'll have the opportunity to jump in!
